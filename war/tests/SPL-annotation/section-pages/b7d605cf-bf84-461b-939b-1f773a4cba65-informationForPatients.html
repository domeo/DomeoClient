17.1 Information for Patients



Patients should use ACZONE
® Gel, 5%, as directed by the physician. ACZONE
® Gel, 5%, is for external topical use only. ACZONE
® Gel, 5%, is not for oral, ophthalmic or intravaginal use.

Patients should not use this medication for any disorder other than that for which it was prescribed.

Patients should report any signs of adverse reactions to their physician.

Protect ACZONE
® Gel, 5%, from freezing.

See Patient Labeling for additional information on safety, efficacy, general use, and storage of ACZONE
® Gel, 5%.
