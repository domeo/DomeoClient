17 PATIENT COUNSELING INFORMATION

See FDA-Approved Medication Guide


Caution patients that pantoprazole sodium delayed-release tablets should not be split, crushed, or chewed.
Tell patients that pantoprazole sodium delayed-release tablets should be swallowed whole, with or without food in the stomach.
Let patients know that concomitant administration of antacids does not affect the absorption of pantoprazole sodium delayed-release tablets.
Advise patients to immediately report and seek care for any cardiovascular or neurological symptoms including palpitation, dizziness, seizures, and tetany as these may be signs of hypomagnesemia [see Warnings and Precautions (5.6)].
Advise patients to immediately report and seek care for diarrhea that does not improve. This may be a sign of Clostridium difficile associated diarrhea [see
