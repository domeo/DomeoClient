Carisoprodol Tablets are available as 350 mg round, white tablets.  Carisoprodol is a white, crystalline powder, having a mild, characteristic odor and a bitter taste.  It is slightly soluble in water; freely soluble in alcohol, in chloroform, and in acetone; and its solubility is practically independent of pH.  Carisoprodol is present as a racemic mixture.  Chemically, carisoprodol is N-isopropyl-2-methyl-2-propyl-1,3-propanediol dicarbamate and the molecular formula is C12H24N2O4, with a molecular weight of 260.33.   The structural formula is:
