4 CONTRAINDICATIONS

NEXIUM is contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to proton pump inhibitors. Hypersensitivity reactions, e.g., angioedema and anaphylactic shock, have been reported with NEXIUM use.
For information about contraindications of antibacterial agents (clarithromycin and amoxicillin) indicated in combination with NEXIUM, refer to the CONTRAINDICATIONS section of their package inserts.
