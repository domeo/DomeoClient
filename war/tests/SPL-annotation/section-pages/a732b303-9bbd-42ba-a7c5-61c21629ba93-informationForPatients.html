Information for Patients (see Patient Package Insert)

Patients and patients' caregivers should be informed of the expected adverse effects of XELODA, particularly nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and hand-and-foot syndrome, and should be made aware that patient-specific dose adaptations during therapy are expected and necessary (see
DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION
). Patients should be encouraged to recognize the common grade 2 toxicities associated with XELODA treatment.
