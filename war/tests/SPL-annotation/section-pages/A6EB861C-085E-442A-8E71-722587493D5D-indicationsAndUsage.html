1  INDICATIONS AND USAGE




Carvedilol is an alpha/beta-adrenergic blocking agent indicated for the treatment of:Left ventricular dysfunction following myocardial infarction in clinically stable patients (1.1)1.1)Hypertension (1.2)1.2)Left ventricular dysfunction following myocardial infarction in clinically stable patients (1.1)1.1)





1.1  Left Ventricular Dysfunction Following Myocardial Infarction
Carvedilol is indicated to reduce cardiovascular mortality in clinically stable patients who have survived the acute phase of a myocardial infarction and have a left ventricular ejection fraction of ≤40% (with or without symptomatic heart failure) [see Clinical Studies (14.1)Clinical Studies (14.1)]. 1.2  Hypertension
Carvedilol is indicated for the management of essential hypertension [see Clinical Studies (14.2)Clinical Studies (14.2)].
