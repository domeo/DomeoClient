4 CONTRAINDICATIONS

SYLATRON is contraindicated in patients with:

A history of anaphylaxis to peginterferon alfa-2b or interferon alfa-2b
autoimmune hepatitis
hepatic decompensation (Child-Pugh score >6 [class B and C])
