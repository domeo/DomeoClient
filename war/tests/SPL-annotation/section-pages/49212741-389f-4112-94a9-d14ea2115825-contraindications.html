CONTRAINDICATIONS

Azathioprine Tablets USP should not be given to patients who have shown hypersensitivity to the drug. Azathioprine Tablets USP should not be used for treating rheumatoid arthritis in pregnant women. Patients with rheumatoid arthritis previously treated with alkylating agents (cyclophosphamide, chlorambucil, melphalan, or others) may have a prohibitive risk of neoplasia if treated with Azathioprine Tablets USP.
