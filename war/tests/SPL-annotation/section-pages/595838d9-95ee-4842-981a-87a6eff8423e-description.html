DESCRIPTIONNortriptyline hydrochloride is 1-Propanamine,
3-(10,11-dihydro-5H-dibenzo[a,d]cyclohepten-5-ylidene)-N-methyl-, hydrochloride.
The structural formula is as follows:




 C19H21N•HCl M.W. 299.8
Nortriptyline hydrochloride is a white to off-white powder, having a slight,
characteristic odor. It is soluble in water and in chloroform; sparingly soluble
in methanol; and practically insoluble in most organic solvents.
Each capsule, for oral administration, contains nortriptyline hydrochloride
equivalent to 10 mg, 25 mg, 50 mg or 75 mg nortriptyline.
In addition, each capsule contains the following inactive ingredients: corn
starch, DandC Yellow #10 aluminum lake, FDandC Blue #1 aluminum lake,
FDandC Blue #2 aluminum lake, FDandC Red #40 aluminum lake, gelatin, iron
oxide black, methylparaben, propylene glycol, propylparaben, shellac glaze,
silicone fluid, sodium lauryl sulfate, and titanium dioxide. The 10 mg, 25 mg
and 75 mg capsules also contain DandC Yellow #10, FDandC Blue #1, and
FDandC Yellow #6.
