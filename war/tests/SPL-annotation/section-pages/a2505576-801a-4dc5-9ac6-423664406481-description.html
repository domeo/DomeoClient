DESCRIPTION

Fluorouracil Injection, USP an antineoplastic antimetabolite, is a sterile, nonpyrogenic injectable solution for intravenous administration. Each 10 mL contains 500 mg fluorouracil; pH is adjusted to approximately 9.2 with sodium hydroxide.
Chemically, fluorouracil, a fluorinated pyrimidine, is 5-fluoro-2,4 (1 H,3H)-pyrimidinedione. It is a white to practically white crystalline powder which is sparingly soluble in water. The molecular weight of fluorouracil is 130.08 and the structural formula is:
