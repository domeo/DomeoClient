CONTRAINDICATIONS

Concomitant use in patients taking monoamine oxidase inhibitors (MAOIs) is contraindicated (see
WARNINGS
).
Concomitant use in patients taking pimozide is contraindicated (see
PRECAUTIONS
).
Citalopram hydrobromide is contraindicated in patients with a hypersensitivity to citalopram or any of the inactive ingredients in citalopram hydrobromide.
